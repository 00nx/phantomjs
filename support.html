Getting Support

Have some questions? Confused by a certain thing? There are many different ways to get help!

Check the FAQ first
Many times, common problems (along with a potential solution) are listed there.
Explore the API
Study the included examples
Post the question to Stack Overflow
Make sure to tag the question with phantomjs.
Post the question to the phantomjs@googlegroups.com user mailing-list
Access this discussion forum easily via Google Groups.

Note that PhantomJS is an open-source project run completely by volunteers. Nobody is paid to provide support and the existing maintenance capacity is already very limited (more details). If nobody offers a possible solution to your problem, consider paying/hiring/contracting a professional software consultant.

Please refrain from from contacting each individual on the development team and asking for their help directly. These individuals already volunteer a considerate amount of their own time, effort, and money on this project. The least we could do is to let them enjoy their remaining spare time with their family and personal life.
